People Serving People

This is the noble cause initiative to revolutionize the way NGOs operate in our society. There are lots of unavoidable obstacles to bringing significant changes in the life of needy people. To solve this problem, we introduce THE SERVICIO, an online public welfare website. A single destination to serve the people at its best with complete honesty and transparency. It provides an extremely easy but impactful unbiased process to implement non-governmental schemes among the most deserving people. It’s just a humble way to express our gratitude to the almighty who provide us with more than we require and blessed us with the opportunity to spread this love to the entire world.
